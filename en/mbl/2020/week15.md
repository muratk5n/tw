Analyzing data as exponential fit.. The formulas below are why we
"take the log" of the $y$ values,

$$
y = a e ^{b x}
$$

$$
\log(y) = \log(a eb x) 
$$

$$
= \log(a) + \log(eb x) = \log(a) + b x
$$

Now the slope of the curve is $b$, the param that defines a particular
exp growth.
